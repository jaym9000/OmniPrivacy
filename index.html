Privacy Policy

Effective: 24 March 2025  |  Last Updated: 21 May 2025

MellowMind Inc. (“Omni,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, the rights you have, and how we keep your information secure when you use the Omni iOS application, website, or any related services (collectively, the “Services”).

1. What Information We Collect

  • Account Information – name, email address, Apple ID, and authentication tokens.
  • Content You Provide – chat transcripts, mood logs, voice notes, journal entries (may include special-category health data).
  • Usage & Analytics – device identifiers, IP address, crash logs, interaction events collected via RevenueCat and our analytics SDKs.
  • Payment Metadata – purchase identifiers and subscription status from Apple In-App Purchase and RevenueCat; we never see your full card details.

2. Legal Bases for Processing

We process your data under one or more of the following:

  1. Consent – explicit consent for processing mental-health data (GDPR Art 9(2)(a)).
  2. Contract – to provide, maintain, and bill for the Services.
  3. Legal Obligation & Vital Interests – to comply with applicable laws, or to protect you or others from serious harm.

3. How We Use Your Information

  1. Deliver personalized AI therapy support and features you request.
  2. Improve, test, and monitor Service performance and safety.
  3. Detect, prevent, and address technical issues or misuse.
  4. Provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  5. Fulfil legal obligations and enforce our policies.

4. Sharing & Transfers

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with trusted processors who act on our instructions:

  • OpenAI LLC – natural-language processing.
  • AWS – secure cloud hosting.
  • RevenueCat Inc. – subscription management.
  • Apple Inc. – in-app purchase processing.

Data may be transferred outside Canada; when we do, we rely on standard contractual clauses or comparable safeguards to ensure protection consistent with PIPEDA.

5. Security Measures

All traffic is encrypted using TLS 1.3 in transit and AES-256 at rest. Access to production systems is restricted by role-based policies and multi-factor authentication. We regularly review our security posture against OWASP recommendations.

6. Data Retention

We retain account data while your subscription is active. Upon deletion, we erase personal data within 30 days unless a longer retention period is required for tax, legal, or compliance reasons.

7. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may:

  • Access, correct, or delete personal data.
  • Export a copy of your data (data portability).
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • Object to certain processing or request restriction.
  • Opt out of analytics or marketing communications.

You can exercise these rights in-app under Settings › Privacy or by emailing omniappofficial@gmail.com.

8. Children’s Privacy

Omni is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, contact us for prompt deletion.

9. International Users

If you access the Services from outside Canada, you consent to the processing and transfer of your information in and to Canada and other countries where our processors operate.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may modify this Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you in-app or by email at least 14 days before the update takes effect.

11. Contact & Complaints

Questions or concerns? Email omniappofficial@gmail.com or write to: MellowMind Inc., 110 Charlotte St, Saint John, NB E2L 2J3, Canada.

If your concerns remain unresolved, you may contact the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada (priv.gc.ca).
