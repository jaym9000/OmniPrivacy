Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. This page explains how Omni AI (operated by a solo developer) collects, uses, and protects your data.

Effective: 24 March 2025 | Last Updated: 21 May 2025

Omni AI (“we,” “our,” or “us”) provides an AI-powered mental-health application and related services (“Services”). This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your information.

  • Account Data – name, email, Apple ID identifier.
  • Content You Provide – chat transcripts, mood logs, voice notes, journal entries (health data).
  • Analytics & Device Data – device ID, IP address, interaction events, crash logs.
  • Payment Metadata – purchase identifiers & subscription status via Apple IAP and RevenueCat (no full card data).
  1. Consent – explicit consent for special-category health data (GDPR Art 9).
  2. Contract – to provide and bill for subscription features.
  3. Legal Obligation & Vital Interests – to comply with law or protect life and safety.
  1. Deliver personalised AI therapy support.
  2. Monitor, debug, and improve performance and security.
  3. Provide customer support.
  4. Detect and prevent fraud or misuse.
  5. Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with processors who help us operate the Services under strict contractual controls:

  • OpenAI LLC – language processing.
  • AWS – secure cloud hosting.
  • RevenueCat Inc. – subscription management.
  • Apple Inc. – in-app purchase processing.

When data is transferred outside Canada, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or comparable safeguards to maintain PIPEDA-level protection.

All traffic is encrypted with TLS 1.3 in transit and AES-256 at rest. Access to production systems is restricted by role-based policies and multi-factor authentication.

We keep data while your account is active. Upon deletion we erase personal data within 30 days, unless longer retention is required by law.

  • Access, correct, or delete your data.
  • Export a copy of your data (portability).
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • Opt-out of analytics or marketing.

Exercise these rights in-app (Settings › Privacy) or email omniappofficial@gmail.com.

Omni AI is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced in-app or by email at least 14 days before they take effect.
