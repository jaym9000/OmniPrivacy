Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. This page explains how Omni AI (operated by a solo developer) collects, uses, and protects your data.

Effective: 24 March 2025
Last Updated: 21 May 2025

Omni AI (“we,” “our,” or “us”) provides an AI-powered mental-health application and related services (“Services”). This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect
  • Account Data – name, email, Apple ID identifier.
  • Content You Provide – chat transcripts, mood logs, voice notes, journal entries (health data).
  • Analytics & Device Data – device ID, IP, interaction events, crash logs.
  • Payment Metadata – subscription status via Apple IAP & RevenueCat (no card data).
2. Legal Bases
  1. Consent – explicit consent for health data (GDPR Art 9).
  2. Contract – to deliver and bill for subscription features.
  3. Legal Obligation & Vital Interests – to comply with law or protect life.
3. How We Use Information
  1. Provide personalised AI therapy support.
  2. Monitor, debug, and improve performance and security.
  3. Offer customer support and respond to queries.
  4. Detect and prevent fraud or misuse.
  5. Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.
4. Sharing & International Transfers

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the following processors under strict contractual controls:

  • OpenAI LLC – language processing.
  • AWS – secure cloud hosting.
  • RevenueCat Inc. – subscription management.
  • Apple Inc. – in-app purchase processing.

For transfers outside Canada, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or comparable safeguards to ensure PIPEDA-level protection.

5. Security Measures

All traffic is encrypted with TLS 1.3 in transit and AES-256 at rest. Production access requires MFA and role-based policies.

6. Data Retention

We retain data while your account is active. Once deleted, we purge personal data within 30 days, unless a longer period is required by law.

7. Your Rights
  • Access, correct, or delete your data.
  • Export a copy (portability).
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • Opt out of analytics or marketing.

Exercise these rights in-app (Settings › Privacy) or email omniappofficial@gmail.com.

8. Children’s Privacy

Omni AI is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy periodically. Material changes will be announced in-app or by email at least 14 days in advance.
