Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Effective: 24 March 2025  |  Last Updated: 21 May 2025

MellowMind Inc. (“Omni,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) provides an AI-powered mental-health application and related services (collectively, the “Services”). This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your personal information when you use our Services.

  • Account Data – name, email, Apple ID identifier.
  • Content You Provide – chat transcripts, mood logs, voice notes, journal entries (health data).
  • Analytics & Device Data – device ID, IP address, app-interaction events, crash logs.
  • Payment Metadata – purchase identifiers and subscription status via Apple In-App Purchase & RevenueCat (no full card data).
  1. Consent – explicit consent for special-category health data (GDPR Art 9).
  2. Contract – to provide and bill for subscription features you request.
  3. Legal Obligation & Vital Interests – to comply with law or protect you or others from serious harm.
  1. Deliver personalised AI therapy support.
  2. Monitor, debug, and improve Service performance and security.
  3. Provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  4. Detect and prevent fraud or misuse.
  5. Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers that help us operate the Services under strict contractual controls:

  • OpenAI LLC – language-processing.
  • AWS – secure cloud hosting.
  • RevenueCat Inc. – subscription management.
  • Apple Inc. – in-app purchase processing.

Where data is transferred outside Canada, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards to maintain PIPEDA-comparable protection.

All traffic is encrypted using TLS 1.3 in transit and AES-256 at rest. Access to production systems is protected by role-based policies and multi-factor authentication.

We keep data while your account is active. Upon deletion we purge personal data within 30 days, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

  • Access, correct, or delete your data.
  • Export a copy (data portability).
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • Opt out of analytics or marketing.

You can exercise these rights in-app (Settings › Privacy) or by emailing omniappofficial@gmail.com.

Omni is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced in-app or by email at least 14 days before they take effect.
